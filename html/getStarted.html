CHALKBOARD

Getting Started

As Chalkboard is a free mobile app that provides students with free notes from professionals in the QCAA system. Students will use their Education of Queensland log-in's to access their account. Their accounts can also be manage by the admin and teachers at the pupil's school, making it an easy solution if a student lost their credentials.


Due to the students using their own accounts, the subjects that are accessible are restricted to their timetable. This prevents students from giving notes to others who shouldn't have access to the course's Education Plan.


As well as a note-taking app, Chalkboard can be used as a social media in terms of communicating or finding tutors that are online or near students. Students will also be able to communicate with eachother and find other users through already knowing another students Education of Queensland username. This is a way to prevent cyberbullying and harrassment amoungst students. Students will also be able to block and report other profiles, the reports will be sent to the student in questions administration.
